About OAE (Oluwatosin Academy of Excellence)

OAE — Oluwatosin Academy of Excellence is a dynamic educational and media brand founded by Mr. Tee, dedicated to empowering learners, inspiring minds, and bridging the gap between classroom learning and real-world application.

We provide academic news and resources for WAEC, NECO, JAMB, and Post-UTME, and are compiling past questions, likely exam topics, and detailed explanations to help students prepare confidently. Our free tutorials cover core subjects and modern skills like coding, AI, and Web3, with ongoing plans for expansion.

Through OAE CONNECTS, we foster a vibrant community where students connect, share knowledge, and support each other. OAE MEDIA TV brings fun to learning by sharing jokes, memes, tech content, and engaging stories, creating a friendly and interactive atmosphere.

OAE TECHTROOPS is our tech-focused community for Full Stack, Cybersecurity, AI, Data Science, and Cloud Computing. It offers expert-led training, hands-on challenges, and resources to help students and tech enthusiasts grow their skills and careers.

TECHTROOPS MISSION:
To empower learners and young professionals with practical tech skills, mentorship, and opportunities for innovation in Africa and beyond.

We are developing tech-driven educational tools like quiz arenas, performance dashboards, learning games, and content browsers, while exploring modern technologies such as AI, automation, and blockchain. Our mission is simple: to make education easier with technology and bring tech to the world.

VISION:
To become a leading African brand in education, innovation, and youth empowerment — blending academic excellence with creativity, technology, and community spirit.

MISSION:
To equip learners and young minds with the knowledge, tools, and confidence needed to excel academically, professionally, and personally.

CORE VALUES:
Excellence • Innovation • Community • Integrity
